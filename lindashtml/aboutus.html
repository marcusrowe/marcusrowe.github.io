ABOUT US

Linda’s is a homey, relaxing, yet energetic Tex-Mex diner that never fails to connect to its roots. Here at Linda’s we make an effort to always share the beauty of the Mexican heritage with our guests. With a menu that ranges from an early breakfast with the family to late night drinks with your friends, Linda’s keeps it authentic and fun! We have designed this restaurant to capture the vibrancy and originality of Día de Muerto, and transform it into a modernized take that everyone can enjoy. So grab your friends, grab your family and take a glimpse into Linda’s, the diner of the underworld!
