Craft Mac & Cheese

1.)What You Need: Grab yourself some elbow macaroni, a good hunk of cheddar cheese, milk, butter, and a sprinkle of flour—nothing fancy, just what you got.

2.)Cook the Pasta: Fill a pot with water, bring it to a boil like you’re heating up a little action. Toss in the macaroni, and cook it until it’s al dente—don’t overdo it, alright? Drain it and put it aside for now.

3.)Make the Cheese Sauce: In the same pot, melt some butter over medium heat. Toss in a tablespoon of flour and give it a good stir, like you’re mixing up a plan. Gradually whisk in the milk, cooking it until it thickens up nicely.

4.) dd the Cheese: Take it off the heat, and start mixing in your shredded cheese. Let it melt like a warm hug, nice and smooth. Season it with a little salt and pepper—just enough to make it sing.

5.)Combine: Now, add that cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce, mixing it all together until every piece is coated in that creamy goodness.

6.)DONE! Dish it up, and enjoy your mac and cheese like a true connoisseur. Mangia!


Ingredients

  • Elbow Macaroni
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 6 cups of water
  • Salt & Pepper
